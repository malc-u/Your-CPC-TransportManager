Services we provide

According to the Statutory Document 3: transport managers Traffic Commissioner identified the following list of the types of activity which might be expected of a transport manager:

  • to manage, audit and review compliance systems to ensure that they are effective;
  • to review any shortcomings such as prohibitions and/or annual test failures;
  • to ensure that relevant changes are notified in accordance with operator licence requirements;
  • to keep up to date on relevant changes in standards and legislation;
  • drivers administration, management and operations;
  • vehicle aministration and management.

Your CPC Transport Manager team is ready to help you comply with law requirements. We are competent professionals who are going to ensure that your vehicles are roadworthy and that your drivers are complying with all relevant rules and legislation. We co-operate with companies from all Operator Compliance Risk Score (OCRS) bands.
