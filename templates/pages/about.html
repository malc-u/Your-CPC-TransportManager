About us

We are small, family run company that is focused on providing transport manager services to small and medium transport operators in Felixstowe and Ipswich area. Smaller companies very often can't afford to employ internal transport manager therefore need support in managing their operator licences. This is something we, as fully qualified CPC transport managers, can provide.

We help in applying for both Restricted and Standard Nationl and International licences. Once they are awarded we help operators maintain them by acting as their External Transport Manager (ETM).

One of our primary job as ETM is to ensure operators are fully compliant. This is, by its very nature quite complex. Some of responsibilities: overseeing vehicle and trailer fleet maintenance and inspections schedule and records, tachograph analysis and defect reporting. We also ensure they comply with all the other undertakings required by their licence.
